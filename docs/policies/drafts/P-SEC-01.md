# P-SEC-01: Policy P-SEC-01

**Status:** DRAFT

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a comprehensive Access Control framework to ensure the security and integrity of organizational data and systems. This policy addresses the critical need for structured access management, which is essential for compliance and governance, as identified in the recent audit.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all agents, including employees, contractors, and systems that interact with or manage organizational data and resources.

## 3. Policy Statement
All access to organizational systems and data must be controlled and monitored to prevent unauthorized access and ensure compliance with security standards.

## 4. Requirements
1. The organization SHALL implement a role-based access control (RBAC) system to ensure that access to data and systems is granted based on job responsibilities.
2. Agents MUST authenticate using multi-factor authentication (MFA) before accessing sensitive systems or data.
3. The system SHALL log all access attempts, including successful and failed attempts, to ensure traceability and accountability.
4. Access permissions MUST be reviewed and updated at least quarterly to reflect changes in job roles or employment status.
5. Agents SHALL complete mandatory security training before being granted access to critical systems.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance with this policy will result in disciplinary actions, which may include revocation of access privileges, agent isolation, or decommissioning, depending on the severity of the violation.
