# P-SEC-01: Policy P-SEC-01

**Status:** DRAFT  
**Effective Date:** TBD  
**Owner:** Office of the CEO

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes the framework for Access Control within the organization to ensure the security and privacy of sensitive data. It addresses identified gaps in governance and compliance, providing a structured approach to managing access to systems and data.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all agents, systems, and personnel within the organization, including contractors and third-party service providers who have access to organizational resources.

## 3. Policy Statement
All agents and systems must adhere to defined access control measures to protect organizational data and resources from unauthorized access and breaches.

## 4. Requirements
1. The organization SHALL implement role-based access control (RBAC) to ensure that access to systems and data is granted based on the principle of least privilege.
2. All access attempts MUST be logged and monitored to detect and respond to unauthorized access promptly.
3. Agents MUST verify the identity of users through multi-factor authentication (MFA) before granting access to sensitive systems and data.
4. The organization SHALL conduct regular audits of access logs to ensure compliance with this policy and identify any anomalies.
5. Systems MUST enforce password complexity requirements and regular password changes to enhance security.
6. Access rights MUST be reviewed and updated at least quarterly to reflect changes in roles or employment status.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance with this policy will result in disciplinary action, which may include agent isolation, access revocation, or decommissioning of systems, depending on the severity of the breach.
