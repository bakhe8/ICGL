## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a comprehensive framework for data privacy within the organization. It addresses the identified gaps in our current documentation, ensuring compliance with governance and regulatory standards. This policy aims to protect sensitive information and maintain the integrity and confidentiality of data handled by the organization.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all agents, employees, contractors, and systems that handle, process, or have access to the organization's data. It encompasses all data management activities within the organization.

## 3. Policy Statement
All agents and systems must adhere to the highest standards of data privacy, ensuring that personal and sensitive information is collected, processed, and stored in a secure and compliant manner.

## 4. Requirements
1. The organization SHALL implement and maintain a comprehensive data privacy program that aligns with applicable laws and regulations.
2. All agents MUST complete mandatory data privacy training annually to ensure awareness and compliance with this policy.
3. Systems handling personal data SHALL employ encryption and access controls to protect data integrity and confidentiality.
4. Agents MUST obtain explicit consent from individuals before collecting or processing their personal data, except where otherwise permitted by law.
5. The organization SHALL conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with data privacy standards and identify areas for improvement.
6. Any data breach or suspected breach MUST be reported immediately to the Data Protection Officer for appropriate action.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance with this policy will result in disciplinary actions, which may include agent isolation, retraining, or decommissioning, depending on the severity of the violation.
