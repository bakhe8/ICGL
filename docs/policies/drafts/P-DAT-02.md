# P-DAT-02: Policy P-DAT-02

**Status:** DRAFT  
**Effective Date:** TBD  
**Owner:** Office of the CEO

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a comprehensive framework for data privacy within the organization. It aims to ensure compliance with relevant data protection regulations and to safeguard sensitive information against unauthorized access and breaches.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all agents, employees, contractors, and systems that handle or process data within the organization.

## 3. Policy Statement
All data collected, processed, and stored by the organization must be protected according to the highest standards of data privacy and security. This includes implementing robust access controls and ensuring data is only used for its intended purpose.

## 4. Requirements
1. The organization SHALL implement access control mechanisms to ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.
2. All data processing activities MUST comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations.
3. Agents and systems SHALL maintain a record of data processing activities, including the purpose and scope of data use.
4. The organization MUST conduct regular audits to assess compliance with this policy and identify potential vulnerabilities.
5. All data breaches or suspected breaches SHALL be reported immediately to the designated data protection officer.
6. Employees and contractors MUST receive training on data privacy and security protocols.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance with this policy will result in disciplinary actions, which may include isolation of the non-compliant agent, revocation of access privileges, or decommissioning of systems involved in the breach.
